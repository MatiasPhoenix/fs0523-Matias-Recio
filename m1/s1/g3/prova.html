Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberriers, and creamy brie. Il will become your new favorite holiday stgarter!

Fresh Cranberrt Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fres or frozen cranberriers

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a sand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is folly absorbed. You dont' need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the vowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
